The Present

Temporal Purgatory

present is purgatory is an interactive 3D digital space that visualizes the present as a liminal dimension between the past and the future. This piece explores the emotional and philosophical tension of being “stuck in time,” grappling with the unalterable nature of the past and the uncertainty of the future. Though the connotation of the name may seem negative, enough time in this 3d space and we may realize that the present is where we are meant to be.

Welcome to purgatory... or should I say the present.

Here are some tips for navigating this space:

1. To rotate, hold down the left mouse button and move.

2. To pan, hold down the right mouse button.

3. To zoom in & out, scroll the mouse wheel.

To enter the space, slowly zoom in until you are inside of the sphere that you will see in front of you.
